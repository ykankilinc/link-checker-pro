# Privacy Policy for Link Checker Pro

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

Link Checker Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.

### 1. Data Collection
We do **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to any external servers. All operations, including link scanning and status verification, are performed locally within your browser and the background service worker.

### 2. Permissions Usage
- **activeTab / tabs:** Used only to scan links on the page you explicitly choose to check.
- **scripting:** Used only to visually highlight links and add status badges on the current page.
- **storage:** Used to store your local settings (e.g., exclusion lists) on your device.
- **Host Permissions (<all_urls>):** Necessary to verify the status of links pointing to external domains.

### 3. Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or trade any information with third parties.

### 4. Security
Your link scan data remains in your temporary browser memory and is discarded when the tab is closed or the scan is reset.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us via GitHub: https://github.com/ykankilinc
